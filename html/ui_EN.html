Pilot School


Welcome to Pilot School

All pilots in Sin City must pass their test before they can fly an aircraft.
Take your time, answer with common sense and don't answer randomly...
You can find the regulations related to aircrafts at sincity.com/regulations

Theoretical test
- The theoretical test costs 35000$, this is not refunded if you fail the test.
- Don't be afraid, the theoretical test can be paid with credits, but be careful not to get into debt.
- If you fail the test the first time, you can't take it again right away, you'll have to take it later.

Practical test
- The practical test costs 45000$, the same as the theoretical test, this payment will not be refunded if you fail.
- Credits are not accepted in this test.
- Make sure you stay alert while driving and avoid accidents!
